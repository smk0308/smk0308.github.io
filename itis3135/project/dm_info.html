Meet The DM

Hi! My name is Erik and I am the Dungeon Master (DM) for the upcoming campaign. I have been running this campaign for about three years now. I've worked with multiple small campaigns to bring the players together to give some finality to their adventures. This has brought together many players to the campaign and put eyes on excitement and fun on playing some good old Dungeons & Dragons.

Being a DM is rewarding and fun. I feel a sense of accomplishment when a group of players completes an objective of a campaign through my story telling and combat scenarios. I get to keep people on their toes with decisions. I will not lie, I do get some chuckles when I get to be evil in my story telling and putting players in precarious situations. It is a lot of fun and I would highly recommend it to any to try and become a DM.

Another side to being a DM is the creation of encounters. I enjoy creating a landscape for the players to move their characters around on and interact with their environment. My story telling gets to let the player be part of the environment.
