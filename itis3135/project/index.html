Campaign Info

The world is in danger. An ancient evil sealed within the core of Faerûn is about to be unveiled. Creatures of mythic proportions roam the lands. Human, dwarven, and elfin lives are at stake. Heroes from all over the world must band together to stop the evil from terrorizing the lands of Faerûn. The cities of Baldur's Gate, Neverwinter, and Waterdeep hold a relic of power that may help our intrepid heroes to ward off the evil plaguing these lands.

As it stands, all three of the relics of power have been found by the heroes of Faerûn. But, at what costs? Many heroes have fallen. The cities have been razed by the cultists that wish to resurrect the ancient evil.

This campaign is a collaborative effort between three separate campaigns being ran concurrently. The entirety of the campaign is to be completed in one glorious overarching campaign. So far, two of the campaigns have completed their task in gather a relic of power. The last has collected their relic of power, but their party was destroyed in the process. A lone survivor of that party will attempt to join the other campaigns and battle the evil.

Meet The DM

Hi! My name is Erik and I am the Dungeon Master (DM) for the upcoming campaign. I have been running this campaign for about three years now. I've worked with multiple small campaigns to bring the players together to give some finality to their adventures. This has brought together many players to the campaign and put eyes on excitement and fun on playing some good old Dungeons & Dragons.

Being a DM is rewarding and fun. I feel a sense of accomplishment when a group of players completes an objective of a campaign through my story telling and combat scenarios. I get to keep people on their toes with decisions. I will not lie, I do get some chuckles when I get to be evil in my story telling and putting players in precarious situations. It is a lot of fun and I would highly recommend it to any to try and become a DM.

Another side to being a DM is the creation of encounters. I enjoy creating a landscape for the players to move their characters around on and interact with their environment. My story telling gets to let the player be part of the environment.
