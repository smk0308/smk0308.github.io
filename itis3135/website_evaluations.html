Web Evaluations

Good Website: Coolors

A website that generatres random color palettes with the click of a button.

  • Contrast: While it has a white background with black text, there is a difference with weight and color in the hierarchy of elements.
  • Repitition: Links repeating at top of page, as well as icons at bottom of landing page.
  • Alignment: Aligned perfectly with some variation to be aesthetically pleasing.
  • Proximity: Makes good use of the space without overwhelming the user.

Bad Website: itcorp

This website is very unfinished, and despite the URL name, they sell bikes.

  • Contrast: White background on pure black text that looks very simple.
  • Repitition: No repition throughout the different pages.
  • Alignment: Everything is aligned to the left with no variation.
  • Proximity: Empty space on every page, nothing done to the margins or padding whatsoever on the landing page.
